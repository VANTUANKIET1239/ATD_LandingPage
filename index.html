Our four guiding principles

We have articulated four guiding principles, in line with our values. They are strengths that define us and empower us to accomplish great things.

They shape how we collaborate within teams, engage with clients, and partner with providers. They inspire each of us to strive for exemplarity.

They are non-negotiable, across every location, activity and entity throughout the Group.

1

Forever Bold

We were born to dare and to build. Our original ambition remains our driving force – a precious legacy and our greatest source of energy.

At CMA CGM, being bold means challenging the status quo, taking nothing for granted. It means thinking big and acting swiftly, because our industries demand agility.

We create our own opportunities. With imagination and the world as our playground, we drive innovation and embrace technology to address the logistical and environmental challenges of tomorrow.
